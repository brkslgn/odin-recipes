Classic Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set them aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until it's browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper to the ground beef. Stir and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  6. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Add half of the ricotta cheese mixture and spread it evenly over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.
  10. Spread half of the cooked ground beef mixture over the mozzarella layer.
  11. Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and ground beef.
  12. Top the lasagna with the remaining marinara sauce and sprinkle the rest of the grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes or until the cheese on top is bubbly and golden brown.
  15. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade classic lasagna!

Tomato Basil Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 slices of baguette or Italian bread
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped basil, and minced garlic. Drizzle with olive oil and mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Toast the slices of baguette or Italian bread until they are lightly crisp on the outside.
  3. Spoon the tomato basil mixture evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  4. Optional: Drizzle a little more olive oil on top for added flavor. Serve the bruschetta immediately as a delightful appetizer or snack. Enjoy!

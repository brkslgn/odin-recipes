Creamy Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork until you achieve your desired level of creaminess. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Toast the slices of whole-grain bread until they are crispy and golden brown.
  4. Spread the creamy avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread slices. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Honey Mustard Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • Wooden skewers (pre-soaked in water to prevent burning)

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the honey and Dijon mustard, stirring well to create the honey mustard sauce.
  2. Thread the chicken cubes onto the pre-soaked wooden skewers, evenly distributing the pieces.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Brush the chicken skewers with the honey mustard sauce on all sides.
  4. Grill the skewers for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Serve hot with additional sauce for dipping.
